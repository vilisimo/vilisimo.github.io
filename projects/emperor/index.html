Emperor

HTMLCSSSass

What

“Emeperor” is a theme for Hugo-based websites. It is primarily aimed at blogs and people who want to showcase their projects.

Why

The main reason to create the theme was lack of themes that really caught my eye. Although Emperor may not be the prettiest theme, it is a theme that I, having created it, know inside out, and that is simple and easy to change. Hence, I have a full control of how it looks.
